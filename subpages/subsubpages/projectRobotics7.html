Short Description:

The Autonomous Underwater Vehicle (AUV) operates autonomously to scan the undersea surface. Through redesigning the existing design, the optimal AUV design was acquired, allowing for higher power efficiency and longer operating time.

Research Topics and Result
  • AUV Hull Redesign

    An efficient design of a hull structure of the AUV provides less drag force against water when travelling underwater. This enables less force needed to boost the entire vessel thus reduces the power consumption to drive. This design produces ~6N of drag force while travelling at maximum of 4 knots.


  • Fluid simulation (ANSYS Fluent)

    This fluid simulation provides great and visualization of how the water interract with entire vessel. The data of pressure and friction drag can be acquired.


  • Motor Selection

    The selection of the motor provides the capability to drive the vehicle based on the force requirements. By simulation and the motor choice, the result would produce the longer underwater operating period by 306% of its initial design.
