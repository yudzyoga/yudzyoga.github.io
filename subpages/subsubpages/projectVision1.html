Webpage: PaveView product page

Description:

Paveview is an AI-powered web application designed for automated road damage detection in Indonesia. Users can upload road video recordings, and the system generates a detailed report highlighting surface defects such as potholes, cracks, and wear, along with an assessment of damage severity. Built with deep learning-based object detection, Paveview streamlines infrastructure monitoring by offering fast, accurate, and location-aware insights tailored to the unique conditions of Indonesian roads.
