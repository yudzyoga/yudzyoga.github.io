Description:

As part of the final project from Computer Graphics 1 at Saarland University, I developeda a non-photorealistic scene which illustrates two different worlds that exist within a same place (combining traditional and modern-like worlds at the same place). All the details of the design process and feature comparisons can be found here (rendering competition website) with the title “To You, 100 Years From Now”. The developed features consists of:

  • Line Tracer
  • Normal Mapping
  • Alpha Masking
  • Area Light
  • Thinlens
  • Bloom effect
  • Halton sampler
  • Image denoising
  • Fog effect
