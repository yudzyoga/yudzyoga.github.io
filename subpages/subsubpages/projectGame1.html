Short Description:

“The Revealers” is our game to compete in MAGE (Multimedia And Game Event) ITS (Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology) 2018. Our game is a 2.5D Stealth-Action game about the corrupt government of a country.


Description:

A stealth-action type mobile game that tells the stories of 5 teenagers trying to reveal the corruption by an underworld company.


Responsibilities:

Develop the entire game and models using Unity3D and Blender3D
