Short Description:

The autonomous wheeled-soccer robots compete in the annual Indonesian Robot Contest (KRI) 2017.

Description:

A Middle size league (MSL) robot soccer competition developed in association with Dagozilla ITB (student robotics team) competing in the Indonesian Robotic Contest (Kontes Robot Indonesia: KRI) held annually by the Ministry of Research, Technology and Higher Education of the Republic of Indonesia. The competition is held with the Robocup Middle Sized League format.

Responsibilities:
  • Developed 3 new mobile soccer robots to compete in the Indonesian Robotics Contest (KRI) 2017 for both regional and national stages.
  • Working closely with another division (mechatronics and sensory) to create breakthrough features.
  • The general development tasks include the design, manufacture, and assembly of each part of the robot.
  • The first time the team built the robots, focusing on mechanical robotic tasks such as design, manufacture, assembly, and analysis for each mechanical part of the robots.

The gameplay of the team

The team competes in the regional and national stages of the annual Indonesian Robotics Contest (KRI) 2018.
