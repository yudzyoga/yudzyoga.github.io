On a dark morning in Future City GraphiTech, Halliday — a treasure hunter — set out to uncover the "Treasure of the past" mentioned in an old book. As he explored a hidden basement in the specified location, he stumbled upon a mysterious trigger. With a sudden jolt, a portal opened before him, leads him into the unknown.
Stepping through the portal, Halliday found himself in an ancient and unfamiliar part of Japan. Surrounded by old structures and unfamiliar landscapes, he felt a surge of excitement. Eagerly, he began his exploration, curious about the thrilling adventure that awaited him in this unknown and distant land.
What exactly is “the treasure”, and could it potentially lead to something more profound? The answer remains uncertain, shrouded in mystery.
